How should I get there?
A few options...
1) Bayard's is quick walk from either Gild Hall or the DoubleTree
2) A variety of subways have stops close by: The 1, 2, 3, 4, 5, N, R, J or Z.
3) Take a cab or car service and instruct them to drop you off at Hanover Square in the Financial District.
What should I wear?
Dress code: fabulous! Please wear something you like and feel fabulous in, whether that be an evening gown or a fabulous t-shirt. That being said, this is an evening wedding in Manhattan, so you can go as fancy as you want. We strongly encourage creative 1920s-1930s attire to fit with our Art Deco theme. If you'd prefer more stolid guidelines, cocktail or gowns for the lady-identifying people and suits and ties for the gentlemen.
For those ladies who'd like to Rent a dream dress you can use the code HARRYANDALICE at Rent The Runway for 15% off.
